# Banned words

AI tells. Use the plainest word that works.

## Tier 1 — kill on sight

These almost never appear in good human prose. If you wrote one, rewrite the sentence.

delve, tapestry, testament, paradigm, synergy, leverage, utilize, elucidate, multifaceted, embark, endeavor, plethora, myriad, realm, catalyze, juxtapose, spearhead, garner, interplay, intricate, enduring, holistic, innovative, seamless, groundbreaking, commences, facilitate, encompass, vibrant, nestled, renowned, showcase

## Tier 2 — suspicious in clusters

Fine on their own. Two or more in one paragraph means rewrite.

robust, foster, enhance, streamline, harness, navigate (figuratively), bolster, underscore, resonate, cultivate, landscape

## Quick replacements

- utilize → use
- facilitate → help, let
- leverage → use
- elucidate → explain
- commences → starts
- encompass → covers, includes
- spearhead → lead
- garner → get, win
- showcase → show
- foster → build, grow
- streamline → simplify, speed up
